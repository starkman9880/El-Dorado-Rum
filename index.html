More About Demerara Distillers

Demerara Distillers Limited, the producers of El Dorado Rum, represent the last vestiges of what was once one of the greatest and most prolific rum countries in the world. As the last remaining distillery in Guyana, DDL carries on the tradition of Demerara Rum, a protected designation that can only be produced here. Through the years they have assembled a collection of 14 very different stills, many from previously revered local distilleries and some representing the only versions of those stills left in the world.  Aged entirely in Guyana, these older rums are extremely rare and concentrated, considering the distillery’s proximity to the equator. After years of pursuing this opportunity, we are thrilled ot finally bring to you, in limited quantities, our 1st vintage cask strength single barrel.
